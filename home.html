Plan Your Trip

From cozy beachfront stays to flavorful local cuisine and unforgettable adventures, explore everything Taniti has to offer and make your trip one to remember.

Lodging

From charming beachfront bungalows to luxurious resorts, Taniti offers a variety of accommodations to suit every traveler. Relax in comfort while enjoying breathtaking island views and warm island hospitality.

Dining

Savor the vibrant flavors of Taniti with fresh-caught seafood, farm-to-table produce, and traditional island specialties. Enjoy everything from casual beachfront cafés with ocean views to fine dining restaurants serving international cuisine.

Activities

Experience the beauty and adventure of Taniti with activities for every traveler. Hike lush rainforest trails, explore an active volcano, or glide through the treetops on a zip-line. Immerse yourself in local traditions with cultural tours and festivals.

About the Island of Taniti

Just 300 miles from the nearest mainland, Taniti has been called “the Pacific’s best-kept secret” for centuries. With less than 500 square miles of untouched beauty, the island offers dazzling white-sand beaches, secluded coves, and a safe harbor that has welcomed travelers since the days of wooden sailing ships.

Beaches & Coastline

Taniti’s coastline features soft white sands, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life. Whether you’re looking to sunbathe, swim, or enjoy water sports, the island’s beaches offer something for everyone.

Rainforests & Volcano

Lush tropical rainforests cover much of Taniti’s interior, leading up to a small active volcano. Hiking trails wind through rich vegetation and offer breathtaking views, making this a must-see for adventurers.

Culture & Cuisine

Experience Taniti’s warm hospitality through its vibrant culture and rich culinary traditions. From local markets to fine dining, the island blends authentic island flavors with international influences.

Activities

Coral Reef Snorkeling

Swim among vibrant coral reefs and colorful tropical fish with a guided snorkeling tour.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Schedule: Daily, 9:00 AM & 1:00 PM
  • Meeting Point: Taniti Harbor
  • Price: $60 per person

Volcano Summit Hike

Take an adventurous trek to the summit of Taniti’s active volcano and enjoy panoramic views.

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Schedule: Tue–Sun, 6:00 AM
  • Meeting Point: Crater Base Camp
  • Price: $80 per person

Rainforest Zipline

Fly above the lush canopy and get a bird’s-eye view of Taniti’s untouched rainforest.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Schedule: Mon–Sat, Every Hour from 8 AM
  • Meeting Point: Merriton Landing Zip Center
  • Price: $50 per person

Scuba Diving Expedition

Dive into deeper waters to explore shipwrecks, exotic marine life, and underwater caves.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Schedule: Wed–Sun, 8:00 AM
  • Meeting Point: Deep Blue Dive Center
  • Price: $120 per person

Coastal Kayaking

Paddle along Taniti’s scenic shoreline, discovering hidden beaches and sea caves.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Schedule: Daily, 10:00 AM & 2:00 PM
  • Meeting Point: Taniti Kayak Club
  • Price: $45 per person

Cultural Dance Show

Experience Tanitian culture through traditional dance, music, and storytelling.

  • Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Schedule: Thu–Sat, 7:00 PM
  • Location: Taniti Cultural Center
  • Price: $30 per person

Deep Sea Fishing Trip

Join local fishermen for a half-day fishing trip and try your luck catching big game fish.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Schedule: Mon–Sun, 6:00 AM
  • Meeting Point: Taniti Fishing Docks
  • Price: $90 per person

Rainforest Nature Hike

Discover exotic plants, wildlife, and hidden waterfalls with a knowledgeable local guide.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Schedule: Daily, 8:00 AM & 1:00 PM
  • Meeting Point: Taniti Eco Tours
  • Price: $40 per person

Sunset Sailing Cruise

Set sail on a luxury catamaran to watch the sun dip below the horizon with complimentary drinks.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Schedule: Daily, 5:00 PM
  • Departure: Taniti Marina
  • Price: $70 per person
